Description

The DateInputBox allows a date to be entered. The date entered may be:

  • an exact date including the day of the week
  • an exact date excluding the day of the week
  • an approximate date
  • a month and a year
  • a year
  • a null index date type which can be defined by the ISV
  • a null date
  • If the date entered is ambiguous, it will be interpreted as having format dd-MM-yyyy. For example, if 01/02/2007 is entered, this will be interpreted as representing the 1st of February 2007 and typically displayed as 01-Feb-2007.
