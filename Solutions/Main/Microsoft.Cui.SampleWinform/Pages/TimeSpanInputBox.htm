Description

The TimeSpanInputBox control allows a duration of time to be entered in a clear and unambiguous manner. The control comprises of a free text entry area where the user can enter a single span of time in a variety of defined formats.

Long units (singular) year month week day hour minute second
Long units (plural) years months weeks days hours minutes seconds
Short units (singular) y m w d hr min sec
Short units (plural) y m w d hrs min sec

TimeSpanInputBox then displays the completely resolved time span according to the pre-defined rules (use y for years, m for months, d for days, hr for hour, hrs for hours, min for minutes, and sec for seconds.)

It allows for the entry of different durations either independently or by using combinations, and allows for the editing of the individual elements of the durations entered into the control.
