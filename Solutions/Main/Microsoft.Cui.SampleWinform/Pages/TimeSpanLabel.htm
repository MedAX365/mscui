Description

The TimeSpanLabel control displays a label representing a span between two times such as the patient's age. The label may include units of years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes and seconds. The units may be expressed in long or short units, with use of plural or singular units determined automatically. The following table provides information on the long and short unit versions and their singular and plural forms.

Long units (singular) year month week day hour minute second
Long units (plural) years months weeks days hours minutes seconds
Short units (singular) y m w d hr min sec
Short units (plural) y m w d hrs min sec
