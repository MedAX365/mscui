Description

The DateLabel control lets you display a date in a clear and unambiguous way. The DateLabel may include:

  • an exact date including the day of the week
  • an exact date
  • a date marked as approximate
  • a month and year
  • a year only
  • a null index date where the exact text displayed can be defined by the ISV
  • a null date

Additional information relating to this control can be found on the corresponding MSCUI control page.
